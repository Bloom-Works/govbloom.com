Charting a digital path

We are at a critical juncture for building digital infrastructure in cities. Technology is changing rapidly, and with it constituent expectations for interacting with their government.

When not done well, digital transformation is costly, it is a liability, and it erodes confidence in government. We work with city and state governments to chart a smart path forward, together.

How We Can Help

We've been in your shoes. Government policies were not designed to make change easy. Rooted in deep first-hand experience, we provide city and state governments with a spectrum of support — from immersive workshops to highly tailored technical assistance.

Guiding Principles

User centered
Tools and experiences should be designed around the needs of users.

Adaptable
Solutions must meet users’ needs today but adapt to a rapidly changing landscape.

Scalable
build with — not for. Teach a man/woman to fish...

Accessible
Everyone is your user, and it’s imperative that your services be accessible to them regardless of the language they speak, any impairments they have, the device they use, etc.

Ever-improving
Observe what works/doesn’t work and adapt (aka Agile). Data informed approach to performance and outcomes.

Open
Document what works and doesn’t work in all the work we do. There’s no reason cities should re-learn each other’s successes and mistakes.
